Packet Capture Forensic Toolkit

Overview: Standardized a process for packet capture, storage, and automated parsing to speed incident response and forensic analysis.

Components

  • tshark-based capture scripts with rotation and metadata tagging.
  • Python parsers to extract flow statistics and suspicious patterns.
  • Storage lifecycle and secure access for long-term retention.

Outcome

  • Faster initial triage — reduced mean time to insight during incidents.
  • Reusable parsers and documented playbooks for incident responders.
