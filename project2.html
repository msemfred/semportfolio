Multi-site BGP Design

Overview: Design and validation of a multi-site WAN using BGP with route-reflectors and conditional advertisement to control route distribution and accelerate convergence.

Key elements

  • Route reflectors to scale BGP sessions and avoid full-mesh complexity.
  • Community tagging and local-preference controls for traffic engineering.
  • Conditional advertisement to limit route propagation during outages.

Validation

  • Lab testing with topologies and failover scenarios.
  • Monitoring integration for BGP state and prefix counts.
